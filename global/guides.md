# Guides


## Custom CSS

1. From `Shopify admin`, navigate to `Online Store → Themes`
1. Click `Customize`
1. Open `Theme settings` and scroll to `Custom code`
1. In the `CSS section`, paste your code

![Custom CSS](/_media/guides/custom-css.png "Custom CSS")
